# Vision & Setting

## Vision

A PvE co-op action MMO where every class plays a fundamentally different game genre. A Gunner plays a first-person shooter. A Vanguard plays a souls-like. A Blade Dancer chains telekinetic combos. All in the same dungeon, fighting the same boss, at the same time.

Target: playable with 5-10 friends first, scalable to thousands later.

NOT a WoW clone. NOT tab-target. NOT traditional tank/healer/DPS trinity. Every point of damage a boss deals should be something a player could have avoided by playing better.

## Setting

Sci-fi military universe. Humanity is organized into an Empire. "Arcanotechnique" is a fusion of magic and technology powered by an energy called "le Flux." Flux is a fundamental physical energy, not mystical magic. A fireball created by Flux produces real heat (RRAD damage), a rock projectile produces real impact (RMEC damage). The source doesn't matter, the physical effect does.

Full lore, history, political context, and world-building are in the TTRPG repository under `/history/`.
