# Resistance System

Three resistance types based on the physical nature of damage, not its source:

| Resistance | Abbreviation | Covers | Examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mechanical | RMEC | Impact, perforation, shockwave | Sword, bullet, rock projectile |
| Radiative | RRAD | Heat, cold, light, electricity | Fire, laser, lightning, frost |
| Internal | RINT | Flux alteration, internal corruption | Necrosis, life drain, corruption |

Formula: `Final Damage = Raw Damage - Applicable Resistance`

A Flux fireball deals RRAD damage. A Flux rock projectile deals RMEC damage. A conventional flamethrower also deals RRAD. The source is irrelevant, only the physical effect matters.

This drives gear choices: players stack resistances based on dungeon content, not just item level.
