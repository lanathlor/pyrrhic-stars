# Long-term Content Vision

NOT for Phase 1-2. These features inform architectural decisions but do not change the immediate plan.

- Space dogfighting: ship flight controller, space zone instances
- Arena PvP: existing combat in dedicated small maps
- Large-scale PvP: hundreds of players, zone sharding, atmosphere-to-ground combat (Planetside 2 inspired)
- Open world zones: terrain-based outdoor areas, 1x1 km+
- Megacity capital: urban hub zone
- Ship flight in atmosphere: landing at designated spots
- Exploration system: skill-based dangerous environment traversal
- Harvesting and crafting systems
- Player market (buy/sell)
