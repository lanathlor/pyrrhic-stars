# Gunner

**Gameplay: First-Person Shooter**

Camera: first person. Input: crosshair aiming, real projectile/hitscan. Core loop: aim, shoot, reposition, use cover.

Flux usage: minimal. Primarily martial with tech gadgets.

| Spec | Identity | Playstyle |
|---|---|---|
| Assault | Aggressive close-mid range | Fast movement, high fire rate. Titanfall energy. See below |

## Assault

Monotarget, constant damage. The skill expression is maintaining maximum uptime while dodging everything.

**Primary fire**: automatic rifle, hitscan, hold trigger and track. This is the baseline DPS.

**Abilities:**

- **Roll** — dodge roll, short cooldown. Core reposition tool.
- **Overclock** — 6-8s window of increased fire rate + move speed. Moderate cooldown. Reward for good tracking during the buff. Not burst — it's a sustained DPS window.
- **Rechamber** — trigger a short animation, hit the button again at the right timing window for a damage buff on the next few seconds of fire. Miss the window and suffer a brief lockout. Weave it in during safe moments between dodges.

**Loop:** shoot → roll through telegraph → shoot → rechamber during safe window → overclock when buffed → repeat. No downtime, no setup, relentless aggression with movement mastery
