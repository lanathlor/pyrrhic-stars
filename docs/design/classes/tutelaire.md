# Tutelaire

**Gameplay: Aura Positioning and Triage**

Camera: third person, slightly pulled back for spatial awareness. Input: aura swapping on GCD, targeted light projections. Core loop: constantly reposition to cover the right allies with the right aura at the right moment.

Flux usage: heavy. Flux commitment between restorative light, radiant light, solid light.

## Core Mechanic

Maintains one active aura at a time, can swap with short GCD. Each aura has a radius and affects everyone inside (allies and/or enemies).

Example auras:

- Damage reduction field (allies take less damage)
- Retribution field (enemies take light damage per tick)
- Cleansing pulse (removes debuffs on activation)
- Rally (movement speed and Flux regen for allies)

On top of auras: targeted light projections (personal shield on ally, light lance on enemy, healing beacon on ground). These cost Flux.

One big cooldown: project a solid light barrier that lasts ~8 seconds. Hero moment ability for blocking one specific boss mechanic.

| Spec | Identity | Playstyle |
|---|---|---|
| Guardian (healer) | Protective support | Stronger healing/shielding auras, healing beacons, Sanctuary burst heal |
| Adjudicator (damage) | Offensive support | Stronger retribution aura, Judgment mark, light lance damage |

A bad Tutelaire stands still with one aura. A good one repositions constantly. A great one pre-positions before mechanics happen.
