# Arcanist

**Gameplay: Tactical Flux Channeling**

Camera: third person, pulled back for spatial awareness. Input: target-based ability usage, Flux management. Core loop: manage Flux throughput, channel at the right moment, avoid interruption.

Flux usage: primary resource. Flux commitment system is central.

| Spec | Identity | Playstyle |
|---|---|---|
| Destroyer | Glass cannon | Huge Flux reserve, massive spells, long channels, vulnerable |
| Battlemage | Melee-range hybrid | Lower Flux, instant combat spells, weaves strikes with arcanotechnique |
| Harmonist (healer) | Flux-based healer | Healing zones and beams, positioning-based healing, not whack-a-mole |

Harmonist healing is channeled, visible, and interruptible like offensive spells. Allies must stand in healing zones or stay near beams. The Harmonist must be protected during channels.

TTRPG source classes: Arcanotechnicien, Arcanotechnicien de Combat, Arcanotechnologue
